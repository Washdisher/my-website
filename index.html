Hobbies and Interests

Outside of my professional pursuits, I’m an avid music producer. I work primarily in electronic, house, techno, DnB, rap, and pop genres, using Ableton as my DAW of choice. My ultimate goal in music production is to release a body of work that truly reflects my creative vision. Though I’ve been in a bit of a creative rut recently, I’m slowly reigniting that spark and looking forward to getting back into the studio.

When I’m not producing music, you’ll likely find me outdoors. I’m passionate about mountain and gravel biking, hiking, and fly fishing. I’m a minimalist at heart, so I prefer investing in high-quality gear that lasts. Whether it’s the durability of a well-worn pair of hiking boots or the precision of a fly rod, I believe in owning fewer, better things that can keep up with my adventures.

Personal Philosophy

I’m a firm believer in the importance of balance. Whether it’s managing my finances, maintaining my fitness routine, or pursuing my creative passions, I strive to lead a life that’s both disciplined and fulfilling. I wake up early, usually around 5 AM, to make the most of my day, and I aim to wind down by 10 PM. Exercise is a daily priority for me, whether it’s hitting the trails on my bike or lifting weights to stay strong.

I’m also on a journey towards self-sufficiency. While I dream of running my own company someday, I’m equally open to other paths that allow me to achieve financial independence and personal fulfillment. Though I’ve faced some setbacks—like a recent breakup and the ongoing challenges of job hunting—I’m committed to staying focused on my long-term goals: to be fit, successful, and, most importantly, happy.

Looking Ahead

As I continue to build my career and explore my passions, I’m excited about what the future holds. Whether I’m producing my next track, landing that dream job, or simply enjoying a peaceful hike, I’m driven by the desire to live a life that’s both meaningful and aligned with my values.
